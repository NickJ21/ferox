About Us

Founded on principles of excellence and precision, Ferox Aero brings a unique blend of military discipline and cutting-edge aerial technology to the drone services industry. Our founder, a former Marine Intelligence officer, and our inspection department head, a certified tower technician and Marine Signal Intelligence/Electronic warfare operator, lead a team committed to delivering the highest standards of service.

Our Mission

At Ferox Aero, we are driven by a mission to provide reliable, secure, and efficient drone-based solutions. Our expertise spans across various sectors, from detailed infrastructure inspections to comprehensive aerial mapping and intricate 3D modeling. We utilize only NDAA/Blue certified drones, ensuring that every project meets stringent security and compliance requirements.

Our People

Our team's Marine Corps background translates into a profound work ethic and an uncompromising approach to precision and quality. With experience in the intelligence community, our team has been trained to strive for excellence under strenuous conditions. This ethos carry over into all aspects of our operations, whether servicing government contracts in the public sector or innovating solutions for private industry challenges.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to upholding the highest standards of safety and excellence. Our commitment to using NDAA/Blue certified drones not only demonstrates our adherence to data security but also our dedication to technological leadership. We believe in the power of technology to transform operations, enhance safety, and deliver impactful data that drives decision-making for our clients.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of drone services, recognized for our integrity, innovation, and ability to execute complex projects with exceptional skill and precision. We strive to expand our reach across both public and private sectors, continually adapting and evolving to meet the needs of a dynamic world.
