At Ferox Aero, we are a veteran-owned UAS mapping business dedicated to providing high-quality, reliable drone services to the construction and agriculture industries. With a strong foundation of discipline, precision, and attention to detail cultivated from our military experience, we specialize in delivering accurate aerial mapping, surveying, and data analysis to help streamline your projects. Whether it's tracking construction progress, assessing land for agricultural use, or creating detailed topographic maps, our cutting-edge drone technology ensures you get the most efficient and effective solutions. We're committed to supporting your success while upholding the values of integrity, service, and excellence that define our business.
We are proud to serve clients in Albuquerque, NM, and San Diego, CA, offering customized services tailored to the unique needs of each region. From construction site monitoring to crop health analysis, our team is committed to supporting your success while upholding the values of integrity, service, and excellence that define our business.
