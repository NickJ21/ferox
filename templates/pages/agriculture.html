In agriculture, drones play a pivotal role in precision farming techniques. They monitor crop health through NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index) imaging, which helps detect plant stress early. Drones also assist in the efficient management of resources by mapping out irrigation patterns and identifying areas that require pest control treatments. By integrating drone data with farm management systems, Ferox Aero helps farmers increase crop yields while reducing operational costs.
